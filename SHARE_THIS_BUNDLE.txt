CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS (FOR ALL AGENTS)
================================================================================

⭐ ANSWER RANDOMIZATION (Required in EVERY lesson)
   - Every Understanding Check question must have randomized answers
   - Use: useMemo + shuffleArray() pattern
   - When: Every component mount
   - This is NON-NEGOTIABLE

⭐ UNDERSTANDING CHECK (Required at SLIDE 4)
   - Every lesson must have 5-question quiz at slide 4
   - Type: 3 scenario + 2 matching questions
   - Content: Test ONLY slides 2-3 concepts
   - Randomize: All answers, always

⭐ DO NOW QUIZ (Required at SLIDE 1)
   - Every lesson must have 5-question quiz at slide 1
   - Content: Questions from PREVIOUS lessons ONLY
   - Purpose: Spaced retrieval (not current lesson content)
   - Format: Mix of topics and types

⭐ SLIDE STRUCTURE (Always 9-10 slides)
   - Slide 0: Title
   - Slide 1: Do Now (retrieval)
   - Slides 2-3: Concepts
   - Slide 4: Understanding Check ⭐
   - Slides 5-6: Simulation
   - Slide 7: Evidence
   - Slide 8: Critique
   - Slide 9: Essay Plan

================================================================================
COMMON MISTAKES
================================================================================

❌ DON'T:
  - Copy questions from another lesson
  - Skip answer randomization
  - Put current lesson content in Do Now
  - Hardcode answer positions
  - Use inconsistent colors
  - Miss slide 4 understanding check
  - Write generic questions

✅ DO:
  - Write lesson-specific questions
  - Randomize ALL answers
  - Make Do Now test previous lessons
  - Shuffle on every mount
  - Use consistent colors
  - Always include slide 4
  - Link feedback to research
