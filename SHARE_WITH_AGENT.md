### ‚≠ê Answer Randomization (Non-Negotiable)
- Use `useMemo` + `shuffleArray()` pattern
- Randomize on EVERY component mount
- ALL options randomized (scenario AND matching)
- Prevents students from gaming the system

### ‚≠ê Understanding Check (Slide 4)
- Exactly 5 questions (3 scenario + 2 matching)
- Test ONLY slides 2-3 concepts
- Detailed feedback (2-3 sentences each)
- Always at slide position 4

### ‚≠ê Do Now Quiz (Slide 1)
- 5 questions from PREVIOUS lessons ONLY
- NO current lesson content
- Spaced retrieval purpose
- No new content on slide 1

These three requirements are reinforced in EVERY document.
